# Brand Policy Companion

Use this file for brand-specific rules that should sit next to the global design standard.

## Purpose

`DESIGN.md` defines the system. `BRAND.md` defines how one brand expresses itself inside that system.

## Include

- logo system rules
- clear-space and minimum-size policies
- approved voice and messaging tone for visual artifacts
- imagery principles
- color usage constraints
- co-branding and partner-lockup rules
- legal or trademark requirements
- market-specific restrictions

## Suggested template

```md
# <Brand Name>

## Positioning
What the brand should feel like across product, marketing, and collateral.

## Logo system
Approved marks, prohibited distortions, clear space, and minimum sizes.

## Color use
Primary roles, accent constraints, contrast expectations, and print handling.

## Typography
Primary and fallback stacks, display rules, small-text constraints.

## Imagery
Photography and illustration principles, visual tone, prohibited treatments.

## Social and campaign rules
Safe zones, headline density, platform-specific exceptions.

## Accessibility
Contrast floors, motion constraints, alt-text expectations, caption policy.

## Governance
Owners, approvers, review cadence, and escalation path.
```
