## Purpose

Use this as the default master logo when horizontal space is available and the company name should be explicit.

## Approved usage

Use it in headers, proposal covers, slide titles, invoices, and email footers.

Maintain clear space equal to the cap height of the wordmark.

## Do not use

- Do not use below 120 px width on screen.
- Do not recolor outside approved theme variants.
- Do not place directly on noisy imagery without a contrast plate.

- light
