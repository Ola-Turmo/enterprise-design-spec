# Nexus Primary Horizontal Logo

The primary horizontal logo is the default mark for all Nexus communications. It combines the wordmark with the N-symbol in a horizontal lockup.

## Purpose

Used as the primary brand identifier across all digital and print contexts where horizontal space is available.

## Approved usage

- Website headers and navigation
- Application title bars
- Marketing materials
- Documentation headers
- Social media profiles
- Business cards and letterhead

## Do not use

- Do not stretch or distort the logo
- Do not change the color outside of approved variants
- Do not add effects (shadows, gradients, outlines)
- Do not use on busy backgrounds without sufficient padding
- Do not rotate or flip the logo
